About Eat 'N' Critic

There are a lot of food delivering apps and Website where you can order food, rate the food and get discounts. And these entire websites are paid or have collaboration with the fancy restaurants. We Team of Eat ‘n’ Critic is trying to build a food community which will be free from all the paid promotions and give our users the best knowledge of the food they wish to have on the other hand.

What is Eat ‘N’ Critic?
It is a non-profitable website planned to build a food community, where any user can post there review about a restaurant.

Why Eat ‘N’ Critic?
While others are focusing on the paid promotions or collaboration with fancy restaurants, we are focused on promoting the local restaurants and street foods on basis of food and services.

How Eat ‘N’ Critic works?
Everybody is welcomed in our website and anybody can visit in search of good and affordable food or restaurants. But if anyone wants to post any review, they need to create an account by using google, facebook or phone number. So the legitimacy of the website remains unharmed. After which the post is analysed by our Sentimental Analytic model and determines that people are happy or not with the respective food and the service of the place. We then create a list of top places as per their reviews and affordances. And use that list to promote those restaurants in our restaurants.

How this is useful or what will be its impact?
People who love food will get affordable and tasty food. People who love food blogging can use this as reference for their next food place blog. Chances of having new food critic are high. Only 100% genuine places will get promoted. We could see thrive in the local restaurants and street food business.

What are the future goals?
Our future goal is to make is available for different locations, Adding new type to data into the website, like pictures and videos. Make it more accurate and efficient results of the analytic model. Make it a standard review system like iMDb, BookMyShow, etc
